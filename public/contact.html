Contact the ReelBoost Team

At ReelBoost, we pride ourselves on maintaining an open line of communication with our community of creators. Whether you have a technical question, need assistance with your account, encounter an unexpected issue on the platform, or simply want to provide feedback on how we can improve our services, our dedicated support team is here to help.

User Support & Technical Assistance

If you are a current user experiencing issues with earning credits, adding Reel URLs, or verifying your email, please do not hesitate to reach out. To help us process your request faster, please include your registered email address and a detailed description of the problem you are facing.

Our Support Hours & Response Times

Our customer support team operates out of the Eastern Standard Time (EST) zone. We are available Monday through Friday, from 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM EST. Operating a large-scale community exchange platform requires significant resources, but we strive to read every single email we receive.

Under normal circumstances, you can expect a response to your inquiry within 24 to 48 hours. During peak periods or major platform updates, response times may be slightly longer. We appreciate your patience and understanding.

Feedback and Feature Requests

ReelBoost was built by creators, for creators, and we are constantly looking for ways to improve. If you have an idea for a new feature, such as a different type of reward system, UI improvements, or integrations with other platforms, please email us! Many of our most popular features originated from user feedback.
