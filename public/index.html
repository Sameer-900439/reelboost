Frequently Asked Questions

How does ReelBoost actually work?

ReelBoost operates on a fair, one-for-one mutual exchange system. It is entirely community-driven. You start by watching Instagram Reels submitted by other verified creators on our platform. For every Reel you watch, you accumulate credits. You can then spend these credits to have your own Instagram Reels watched by the community. It is a completely free, symbiotic cycle of engagement.

Is it safe for my Instagram account?

Yes, absolutely. We do not ask for your Instagram password, nor do we require you to link your Instagram account directly via API. We only need the public URL of the Reel you want to promote. Furthermore, because all views are generated organically by real human users navigating to your link, there is no risk of your account being flagged or banned by Instagram for suspicious bot activity.

Why doesn't ReelBoost use bots?

Bot views are "empty calories" for social media growth. Instagram's algorithm easily detects and ignores fake engagement, which can actually harm your account's standing. By insisting on a community of exclusively real, verified human beings, we ensure that the engagement you receive actually counts toward your organic reach and algorithmic growth.

Is the service completely free?

Yes, the core functionality of ReelBoost is 100% free to use. You earn credits with your time by supporting other creators. You never have to spend money to get views. We sustain the platform through optional, non-intrusive advertising in order to keep servers running and the service absolutely free for everyone.
